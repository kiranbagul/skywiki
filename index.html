Features

Time Travel

Do you know how sky shifts every second ? As the earth revolves around its axis, it unravels and blankets celestial bodies from our view. Get the glimpse of the sky by traveling in time. You will be able to track motion of messier objects, brightest stars, solar system planets and constellations.

Periscope

Includes rise/set information about sun, moon, planets and events for the day. Includes path tracker for sun and moon.

Calendar

Never miss an important astronomy event now. Scroll through the calendar to get the important upcoming events about Lunar Phases, Eclipses, Equinoxes/Solstices, Moon Apogee/Perigee, Moon-Planet Conjunctions, Planetary Events and Meteor Showers.

Astronomy News

One stop center for the all information on major events and news as they happen, here you can find aggregated astronomy news from all the popular feeds.

Gallery

Amazing photos of the celestial bodies that eyes never get a chance to cherish. Swipe through spectacular photos released by major space agencies everyday, taking your curiosity to a new level.

Compass

Orient yourself in the desired direction with this feature.
